VISA CLASSIC / MASTERCARD GOLD — GENERAL CONSUMER TERMS
Source: visa.com/en_GB/legal and mastercard.com (accessed March 2026)
Cards: Visa Classic, Mastercard Gold

VISA CLASSIC
- Annual fee: typically £0–£25 depending on issuing bank
- Zero liability on unauthorised transactions
- Chargeback available within 120 days
- Foreign transaction fee: typically 2.75–2.99%
- Contactless limit: £100 per transaction (UK), €50 (EU)
- No travel insurance on Classic tier
- No airport lounge access on Classic tier

MASTERCARD GOLD
- Annual fee: typically £60–£120 depending on issuing bank
- Cashback: typically 0.5%–1% on purchases
- Foreign transaction fee: varies, some Gold cards fee-free
- Travel insurance: included on most Gold cards
  (medical up to £5M, trip cancellation up to £5,000)
- No airport lounge access on Gold tier
- Zero liability standard on all Mastercard products
- Chargeback available within 120 days

BOTH CARDS
- Chip and PIN: EMV standard compliant
- 3D Secure: Visa Secure / Mastercard Identity Check
- Card freeze available via issuing bank app
