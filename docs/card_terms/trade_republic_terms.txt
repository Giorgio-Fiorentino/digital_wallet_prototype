TRADE REPUBLIC ACCOUNT — OFFICIAL TERMS SUMMARY
Source: traderepublic.com/en-de/legal (accessed March 2026)
Card represented as: Trade Republic

ACCOUNT TYPE
- Licensed EU bank (BaFin regulated)
- Combined investment and spending account with Visa debit card

INTEREST ON CASH
- 3.25% per year on uninvested cash (variable, as of 2025)
- Calculated daily, paid monthly
- Up to €50,000 covered by German deposit guarantee scheme

CARD SPENDING
- No foreign transaction fees worldwide
- No ATM fees up to 5 withdrawals/month, then €2 each
- Instant spending notifications

CASHBACK
- 1% on all card purchases, credited as fractional shares
- Maximum €15 cashback per month

INVESTMENT
- Commission-free stock and ETF trading (€1 settlement fee)
- Savings plans from €1/month
- Crypto trading available

FEES
- No monthly account fee
- No card issuance fee
- No inactivity fee
