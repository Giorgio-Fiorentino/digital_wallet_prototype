REVOLUT ACCOUNT — OFFICIAL TERMS SUMMARY
Source: revolut.com/legal/terms (accessed March 2026)
Card represented as: Revolut

ACCOUNT PLANS
- Standard (free): basic features, limited free ATM withdrawals
- Plus (£3.99/month): enhanced support, purchase protection
- Premium (£7.99/month): overseas medical insurance, priority support
- Metal (£14.99/month): 1% cashback outside Europe, 0.1% inside Europe,
  premium concierge, airport lounge access

ATM WITHDRAWALS
- Standard: free up to £200/month, then 2% fee
- Premium: free up to £400/month
- Metal: free up to £800/month

CURRENCY EXCHANGE
- Standard: fee-free up to £1,000/month during market hours, then 0.5%
- Weekends: 1% markup on all plans
- Premium/Metal: unlimited fee-free during market hours

FOREIGN TRANSACTION FEES
- No foreign transaction fees on card payments on any plan

FRAUD PROTECTION
- Disposable virtual cards for online purchases
- Instant freeze/unfreeze via app
- Real-time push notifications
- Zero liability on unauthorised transactions reported promptly

INTEREST ON SAVINGS
- Up to 4.75% AER on Savings Vaults (Premium/Metal, variable, GBP)
