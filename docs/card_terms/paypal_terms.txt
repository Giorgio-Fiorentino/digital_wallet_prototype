CURRENCY CONVERSION
- 3% above base exchange rate when PayPal converts
- 1.5% cross-border fee if merchant converts

BUYER PROTECTION

WITHDRAWALS
- Standard to bank: free, 1-3 business days

FRAUD PROTECTION
- Unauthorised transaction protection if reported promptly
- 24/7 fraud monitoring
- Two-factor authentication available
